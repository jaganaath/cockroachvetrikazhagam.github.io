Government Schemes for Women — Tamil Nadu

Kalaignar Magalir Urimai Thogai

1.06 Cr

₹1,000/month direct transfer to women heads of household. Targets economic independence for women.

Pudhumai Penn Scheme

3.4 Lakh

₹1,000/month scholarship for government school girl students who join higher education. Reduces dropout after school.

SHG — TNSRLM

63 Lakh

4.6 lakh Self-Help Groups with 63 lakh members. Microfinance, skill training and enterprise support for rural women.

Makkalai Thedi Maruthuvam

1.2 Cr

Doorstep health and nutrition services particularly benefiting women who cannot access hospitals easily.

Cradle Baby Scheme

43,000+

Accepts unwanted girl babies since 1992 — preventing female infanticide. Over 43,000 babies have been cared for.

CMCHIS — Women's Health

1.59 Cr families

₹5 lakh annual health cover per family for 1,015+ surgeries. Maternal and obstetric surgeries fully covered.
