The Honest Verdict — Credit & Blame
DMK — Credit
  • ✓Introduced 100-unit free electricity (2006) — now a pan-India benchmark
  • ✓Best AT&C loss reduction in recent history: 18.2% → 14.8% (2021–24)
  • ✓#1 rooftop solar rank nationally under 2021–26 DMK government
  • ✓Near-zero urban load shedding — most reliable supply in TN history
  • ✓Green Energy Corridor II — ₹13,000 Cr grid investment underway
DMK — Blame
  • ✗2006–11: No tariff revision for 5 years → built the structural revenue gap
  • ✗2010–11 crisis: 8–12 hr rural load shedding — worst in TN history
  • ✗2021–26: Accumulated losses still rising despite efficiency gains
AIADMK — Credit
  • ✓2011–16: Added 9,120 MW capacity — largest ever in any 5-year period
  • ✓2011–16: Added 7,400 MW renewable — transformed TN's energy mix
  • ✓Tariff revisions 2012 & 2015 addressed revenue gap (politically unpopular but necessary)
  • ✓Reduced load shedding drastically — urban consumers got relief
AIADMK — Blame
  • ✗Accumulated losses grew from ₹11K Cr to ₹74.7K Cr across both terms (+580%)
  • ✗2016–21: AT&C losses INCREASED from 17.2% to 18.2% — worst outcome
  • ✗Highest ever annual loss (₹9,200 Cr) in 2020-21
  • ✗Smart meter rollout stalled; CAG flagged coal procurement irregularities
